Florent Bartoccioni

I am PhD student supervised by Patrick Pérez from Valeo.ai and Karteek Alahari from INRIA Thoth team.

Before starting my PhD, I did a master in computer science at École normale supérieure de Rennes. I am now working on autonomous driving systems, fully commited to make cars safer. In particular, I am working on 3D perception, dynamic scene understanding and world models.
